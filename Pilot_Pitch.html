The Audit Problem

In recent 2025/2026 oversight reviews, state and federal auditors have increasingly scrutinized county justice departments. Departments are failing audits and exposing themselves to civil liability, not due to poor supervision practices, but due to fragmented, disjointed paper trails.

Current technical check-in procedures place an immense logistical burden on participants, leading to missed check-ins that consume thousands of hours of officer administrative time and drain county incarceration budgets.

The COMPLAVI Documentation System

COMPLAVI is not a tracking system. It is a secure documentation system. The platform is designed to reduce administrative overhead while creating secure, verifiable audit records that support oversight and legal review.

  • Phone Based Identity Check: Participants complete mobile check-ins using secure, phone based identity checks and GPS confirmation. Each check-in is completed in seconds using the device they already carry.
  • Real-Time Audit Recording: Once a check-in is verified, the event is timestamped and securely recorded within the COMPLAVI audit system, creating a continuous and traceable compliance history.
  • On-Demand Audit Reporting: Officers and administrators can generate structured compliance reports instantly, providing clear, organized documentation designed to support audit requirements and court proceedings.
