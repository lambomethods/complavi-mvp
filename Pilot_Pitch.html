The Audit Problem

In recent 2025/2026 oversight reviews, state and federal auditors have increasingly scrutinized county justice departments. Departments are failing audits and exposing themselves to civil liability, not due to poor supervision practices, but due to fragmented, disjointed paper trails.

Current technical check-in procedures place an immense logistical burden on participants, leading to missed check-ins that consume thousands of hours of officer administrative time and drain county incarceration budgets.

The COMPLAVI Infrastructure Solution

COMPLAVI is not an active tracking mechanism. It is a Secure Audit Ledger. We eliminate administrative overhead while guaranteeing equitable supervision.

  • Secure On-Device Biometric Verification: Participants check in using our mobile client. Utilizing the same cryptographic locks as consumer banking applications, they secure their identity and exact GPS coordinates in 30 seconds.
  • Instant Ledger Verification: The moment the biometric gate clears, the event is timestamped and locked onto the Live Matrix interface.
  • One-Click Audit Reporting: When an auditor or oversight judge demands compliance documentation, officers click one button to generate a certified, secure PDF ledger.
